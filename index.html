Mike Kurtz

About

Mike Kurtz is Senior Product Manager at the social media analytics firm, Crimson Hexagon.

Since arriving at Crimson Hexagon, Mike has held positions in both Client Services and Product Management, where he has led the succesful launch of innovative new products.

Outside of work, Mike is an active musician and songwriter. Mike currently lives in in Somerville, MA, where he can be found playing an open mic, chearing on his all-star kickball team, or roaming the streets of Boston with a bubble tea in hand.
